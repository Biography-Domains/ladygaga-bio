Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta (born March 28, 1986), most famously known as Lady Gaga, is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and businesswoman. Born and raised in New York City, she initially studied at Convent of the Sacred Heart and later attended New York University's Tisch School of the Arts before dropping out to pursue a music career. Gaga began performing in the rock music scene of New York City's Lower East Side and enrolled at Collaborative Arts Project 21, through which she continued honing her songwriting skills, and soon after she signed with Streamline Records by the end of 2007. Her debut studio album, *The Fame* (2008), was a critical and commercial success, spawning the international hit singles "Just Dance" and "Poker Face." The Fame was later reissued as *The Fame Monster* (2009), which contained the equally successful singles "Bad Romance," "Telephone," and "Alejandro." Gaga's flamboyant image, coupled with her genre-bending musical style, quickly established her as a global pop icon. Her subsequent albums, *Born This Way* (2011), *Artpop* (2013), *Cheek to Cheek* (2014) (a collaborative jazz album with Tony Bennett), *Joanne* (2016), and *Chromatica* (2020), explored diverse musical avenues, ranging from electronic dance music to pop, rock, jazz, and country. Beyond her music career, Lady Gaga has also achieved success as an actress, winning a Golden Globe Award for her role in *American Horror Story: Hotel* (2015–2016) and garnering widespread acclaim for her performance in the film *A Star Is Born* (2018), for which she received an Academy Award for Best Original Song (“Shallow”). Gaga is known for her boundary-pushing artistic vision, extravagant costumes, and powerful vocals. Her music often addresses themes of individuality, acceptance, self-empowerment, and social justice. She has been lauded for her philanthropic work, particularly her advocacy for LGBTQ+ rights through her Born This Way Foundation. With over 170 million records sold worldwide, Lady Gaga stands as one of the best-selling music artists of all time, and she continues to be a major force in the entertainment industry.
