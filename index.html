Lady Gaga

Pop, Electronic, Dance-pop, Jazz, Rock, Disco

Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta (born March 28, 1986), most famously known as Lady Gaga, is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and businesswoman. Born and raised in New York City, she initially studied at Convent of the Sacred Heart and later attended New York University's Tisch School of the Arts before dropping out to pursue a music career.
